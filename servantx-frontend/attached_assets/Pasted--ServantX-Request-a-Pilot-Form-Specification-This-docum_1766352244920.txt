# ServantX — Request a Pilot (Form Specification)

This document defines the **exact copy, fields, and UX guidance** for the ServantX **Request a Pilot** page.
It is designed to be **CFO-safe, low-friction, and non-salesy**.

---

## Page Purpose

Allow hospital CFOs and Revenue Integrity leaders to request a **limited-scope pilot** without entering a sales funnel.

Tone:

* Measured
* Professional
* Time-conscious
* Executive-grade

---

## Page Header

### Title

**Request a Limited-Scope Pilot**

### Subheadline

Evaluate ServantX Intelligence on a focused, contract-backed underpayment review — with no upfront cost.

---

## Intro Copy

This pilot is designed to validate whether deterministic underpayment recovery applies to your organization.

Scope is limited, findings are auditable, and participation does not require changes to your billing workflows.

---

## Form Fields

### 1. Organization Information

**Hospital / Health System Name**
Text input (required)

**State / Region**
Text input or dropdown (required)

---

### 2. Contact Information

**Primary Contact Name**
Text input (required)

**Title / Role**
Dropdown (required)
Options:

* CFO
* VP Finance
* Revenue Integrity Director
* Revenue Cycle Leader
* Compliance
* Other

**Work Email Address**
Email input (required)
Helper text: We do not use personal email addresses.

**Phone Number**
Text input (optional)

---

### 3. Hospital Profile

**Approximate Annual Net Patient Revenue**
Dropdown (required)
Options:

* Under $20M
* $20M–$40M
* $40M–$75M
* $75M+

**Hospital Type**
Checkboxes (select all that apply)

* Rural
* Critical Access
* Community Hospital
* Regional Health System

---

### 4. Pilot Interest & Scope

**Primary Area of Interest**
Checkboxes (required)

* Outpatient facility claims
* Modifier-related underpayments
* Contract misapplication
* Not sure (seeking assessment)

**Primary Payer(s) of Interest**
Text input (optional)
Helper text: e.g., commercial payer or dominant plan

**Timeframe for Pilot Review**
Dropdown (required)
Options:

* Last 90 days
* Last 180 days
* Open to recommendation

---

### 5. Governance & Readiness

**Do you have internal approval to explore a limited-scope pilot?**
Radio buttons (required)

* Yes
* Not yet
* In progress

**Preferred Next Step**
Radio buttons (required)

* Introductory conversation
* Technical overview
* Written pilot outline

---

### 6. Optional Context

**Anything else we should know?**
Multiline text (optional)
Helper text: This may include payer behavior, prior audits, or internal priorities.

---

## Submit Section

### Primary Button

**Request Pilot Review**

### Supporting Text (Muted)

No upfront cost. No obligation.
All findings are contract-backed and require your approval before any submission.

---

## Post-Submission Confirmation

### Message

**Thank you.**

A member of the ServantX team will review your request and follow up with next steps.

This review is exploratory and does not obligate participation.

---

## UX / Design Notes

* Single-column layout
* Generous spacing
* No progress bar
* No urgency language
* No marketing language
* No file uploads
* Footer remains minimal

---

## What Not to Include

* No pricing
* No scheduling automation
* No sales language
* No gated content

This page captures **intent**, not onboarding.

---

## Footer Note (Optional)

ServantX is a Servant Health company and a Disabled Veteran Owned Business.
